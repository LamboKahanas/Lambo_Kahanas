Programming Workshop

Our Introduction to Coding Workshop was a huge success, providing participants with a solid foundation in programming. Throughout the session, attendees learned the basics of coding, including key concepts like variables, loops, and conditionals, and got hands-on experience with popular programming languages such as Python, HTML/CSS, and JavaScript. Whether they were complete beginners or aspiring developers, everyone had the opportunity to write, test, and debug their own code, gaining practical skills and confidence in their abilities. The workshop fostered a collaborative and supportive environment, where participants learned from each other and received guidance from experienced instructors. By the end, everyone walked away with a clear understanding of how to approach coding challenges and the tools to continue learning on their own. Thanks to everyone who joined us, and we look forward to offering more opportunities to explore the world of coding in the future!
